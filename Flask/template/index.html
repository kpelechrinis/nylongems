What is NylonGems?

Have you ever gone to a new gym to hoop only to realize that the nylon is not of your liking? I sure have; especially when they have these tight nylon with no fluidity that the ball barely fits through. This made me think what do other people like when they play? I gathered some images from different types of basket nets and every time the page is loaded you will see two of them randomly picked. You can choose one of the two and we will collect these data. We will then process them using pairwise comparisson models to provide a probabilistic ranking of preference. Gyms now will now what are the best options!

Vote

You can vote as many times as you want. Every time you vote a new pair of randomly choosen nylons will come up. If you cannot decide between two images , just refresh the page and two new ones will come up!
